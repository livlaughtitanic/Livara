Cookie Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how Livara uses cookies and similar technologies on our website.

What are cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help websites function properly and can provide information about how the site is used.

How we use cookies

Our website uses minimal cookies, primarily to ensure the site functions correctly and, where applicable, to understand how visitors use the site so we can improve it.

TypePurpose
EssentialRequired for the website to function properly
AnalyticsHelps us understand how visitors use the site, where used

Managing cookies

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies or alert you when cookies are being sent. Please note that disabling cookies may affect how the website functions.

Contact
