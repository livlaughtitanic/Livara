Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Livara ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses and protects information when you visit our website or contact us about our services.

Information we collect

When you fill out our contact form, we collect the information you provide, which may include your name, email address, business name, website and any details you share about your business.

How we use your information

  • To respond to your enquiry and discuss our services
  • To provide proposals, audits or reports you have requested

How we store your information

Enquiries submitted through our contact form are processed using Formspree. We do not sell, rent or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Your rights

You can ask us to access, correct or delete any personal information we hold about you at any time by emailing liv@livara.ie.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at liv@livara.ie.
